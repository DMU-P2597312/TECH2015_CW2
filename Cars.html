Octane

As a whole the Octane is one of the most popular cars due to its versatility, Hitbox and smooth handling. It proves as one of the default cars to be a fan favourite especially in tournaments and championships. Lots of people reccomend this car for beginners as it is light and mobile.

The Batmobile

The batmobile as a top 4 would be seen as quite unusual do to its body shape but mostly it is just a fan favourite in design and weight as like some cars this carries a lot of weight behind it which means it might hit the ball harder than most other cars.

The Dominus

The Dominus is a car that can take corners very well and has the original hitbox for 20 unlike other cars in rocket league. Its slight bump at the front makes its car a lot more top heavy and can prove to be very useful when using air dribbles (controlling the ball in the air).

Breakout

The breakout has a very slick front and can slide under the ball easily. This makes it better with ball dribbiling on the ground as its flat surface can carry the ball across the pitch with ease as long as you keep central balance.
